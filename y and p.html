Yoga and Pilates

Welcome to Yoga and Pilates Classes!

Discover the benefits of Yoga and Pilates for flexibility, strength, and mindfulness. Our gym offers a variety of classes led by experienced instructors to help you enhance your physical and mental well-being.

Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama). It aims to balance body and mind through gentle movements and relaxation.

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa Yoga is a dynamic style that synchronizes breath with continuous flowing movements. It helps build strength, flexibility, and coordination.

Pilates Matwork

Pilates Matwork focuses on core strength, stability, and body awareness. It involves a series of exercises performed on a mat to improve posture and overall body control.
