Strength Training

Welcome to Strength Training!

Strength training is essential for building muscle, improving strength, and boosting metabolism. Our gym offers a wide range of strength training exercises and equipment to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Barbell Squat

The barbell squat is a compound exercise that targets the lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. It also engages the core for stability.

Dumbbell Bench Press

The dumbbell bench press is a chest exercise that helps strengthen the pectoral muscles. It also engages the shoulders and triceps as secondary muscles.

Deadlift

The deadlift is a compound exercise that targets multiple muscle groups, including the back, glutes, and hamstrings. It also engages the core and grip strength.
